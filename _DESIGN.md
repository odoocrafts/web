# Odoocrafts Website Redesign

## Vision

Create a world-class digital experience for Odoocrafts that feels closer to Stripe, Linear, Framer, Vercel, Apple, and Spline than a traditional ERP company website.

The website should instantly communicate:

- Premium
- Technical excellence
- Innovation
- Trust
- Automation
- Global capability

This should not feel like an Odoo partner website.

This should feel like a next-generation technology company that happens to specialize in ERP, CRM, AI, Automation and Digital Transformation.
